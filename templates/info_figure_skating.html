Figure Skating @ OIC

Figure skating is available at the Ozaukee Ice Center for kids and adults of all ages. You may sign up for an OIC Web Apps account prior to skating. Once you have registered for an account, you may use the Figure Skating Web App to sign up and pay for open figure skating sessions.

Parents and guardians may sign up for an account and register children under the age of 18 for open figure skating sessions.
