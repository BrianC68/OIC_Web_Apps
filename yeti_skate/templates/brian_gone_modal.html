Pre-Skate Open Instructions

North Rink

Rink lights are located in the north rink compressor room, accessed through the north rink zamboni room door. Go through the door, turn left, go through double doors on left. Room lights will turn on. Rink lights are located by the door to the hallway. Turn on all rink lights and hallway light. There is an electrical panel marked "scoreboard", open it and flip breaker #4 to turn on the scoreboard. Then put nets out and close zamboni doors, left door first. If the scoreboard does not have the clock up, just hold the power button on the scoreboard controller in the scorers box till the scoreboard turns off. It will come back on with the clock showing.

South Rink

Rink lights are located in the south rink zamboni room. Go through the south rink zam room door and turn left, rink lights are marked "50%", push them both up. Turn on locker room hall switch. Then go in south rink compressor room door on the left and turn left. The electrical panel has the scoreboard breakers, #26 & #28, just flip the breakers to turn on the scoreboards. Put nets out and close zamboni doors, left door first. If the scoreboard does not have the clock up, just hold the power button on the scoreboard controller in the scorers box till the scoreboards turn off. They will come back on with the clock showing.
