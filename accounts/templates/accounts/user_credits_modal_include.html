User Credits

Tired of entering your credit or debit card information? Purchase Credits!

You may purchase credits to use toward skate sessions. If you purchase credits, your skate sessions will be deducted from your credit balance. You may purchase credits at any time.

Purchasing credits is optional. If you don't use credits, the process will remain the same. Just pay for skate sessions at the time you register.
